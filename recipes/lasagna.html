Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a dish made of flat pasta sheets layered with sauce, cheese, and other ingredients, then baked.
The pasta sheets are wide and flat, and can have straight or curly edges.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Ground Beef

Steps

  • Boil the noodles
  • Make the sauce
  • Assemble the layers of the pasta, sauce, and other ingredients
  • Bake until bubbly
