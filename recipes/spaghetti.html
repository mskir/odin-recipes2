Spaghetti

Description

Filipino Spaghetti is a sweet version of the beloved pasta dish. This popular Filipino favorite is usually served during holidays and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Ground Pork
  • Hotdogs
  • Grated Cheese

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients
  2. Cook and drain your spaghetti
  3. Mince the veggies and aromatics
  4. Saute ground sausage, hotdogs, and veggies in a wok
  5. Add sauce and milk
  6. Toss it all together
